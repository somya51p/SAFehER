About Us

SAFehER is a platform where pregnant women’s physical and mental safety is ensured. It is a community of women, doctors, NGO workers, and psychologists where pregnant ladies are protected from all kinds of physical and mental traumas. Today, when we talk about women's safety, it is not only restricted to the safety of women walking on a street but also the violence and abuse they have been subjected to in their own house. This proves to be dangerous especially for pregnant women who are pressurized for forceful abortion or are subjected to abuse. This platform is specifically designed for pregnant women to help them resolve their problems that could prove insidious to the child and to get detailed information about pregnancy.

Besides providing physical and mental safety to pregnant ladies, the site is quite informative about everything related to pregnancy and about what women like to read or perform during this tenure. Different magazine links are provided for the same. Do you know what is special about this? Users can also add the magazines they like to read. Uploading of any content or registration of users into the site or allowing NGO helpers, doctors, psychologists to assist the users, the admin will keep a check on all these. After the admin will verify, then only users can upload anything on the site. Also, the admin will immediately remove anyone trying to violate discipline or writing inappropriate messages or content. Admin will keep a check on everyone to ensure security. BMI and menstrual cycle check can also be done from time to time to ensure everything is going right. This website has everything needed to ensure pregnant ladies physical and mental safety.




How it works

Along with physical safety, ensuring the mental safety of a pregnant woman is equally important. In the website, there are all sorts of assistance provided to solve even the smallest doubt related to pregnancy. One can easily ask queries from doctors regarding the problems they may have been facing or can ask for tips to ensure the health of both mother and child. One cannot always meet doctors to discuss any small abnormality or share their emotions about how they are feeling, here women can chat with doctors as well as from each other to lighten their mood and share their experiences and emotions. In a lively and healthy environment, mental peace will be established.

SAFehER serves as a platform where females can come together and find other females with similar interests in sports, ultimately increasing their participaton in the community sport. This will not only help them to be mentally and physically fit, but will also help them meet different women and empower each other.
